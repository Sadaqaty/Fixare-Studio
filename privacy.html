Privacy policy

Your data, protected with intention

Fixare Studio respects your privacy and is committed to operating transparently. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and the safeguards we apply while building digital products for our clients.

Effective date 07 December 2025
Last updated 07 December 2025

1. Who we are

Fixare Studio ("Fixare", "we", "our" or "us") is a creative technology agency delivering brand, product, and systems solutions for companies worldwide. We operate from Pakistan with remote collaborators in Australia, the United States, and the European Union.

You can reach our privacy team at privacy@fixare.studio.

2. Data we collect

We only collect data that helps us serve you better. This may include:

  • Contact information such as name, company, email address, and phone number when you submit a form or book a meeting.
  • Project context including goals, requirements, files, and feedback you voluntarily provide to scope a solution.
  • Usage data like IP address, device type, browser, and interaction metadata collected through analytics tools to secure our systems and improve experience.
  • Transactional data tied to proposals, invoices, or service contracts.

We do not intentionally collect sensitive personal data unless it is essential for a specific engagement and you explicitly consent.

3. How we use your data

  • Responding to your inquiries, demo requests, and partnership discussions.
  • Preparing proposals, contracts, and delivery plans tailored to your product needs.
  • Operating, maintaining, and securing our websites, collaboration tools, and infrastructure.
  • Sending essential communications related to project milestones, billing, or legal notices.
  • Improving our services by analyzing aggregated usage patterns and feedback.
  • Complying with legal obligations, dispute resolution, and enforcement of agreements.

4. Legal bases for processing

We rely on the following legal bases under GDPR and comparable frameworks:

  • Performance of a contract when delivering services you request.
  • Legitimate interests for operating a secure, reliable business platform.
  • Consent for optional marketing updates or beta research (you can withdraw anytime).
  • Legal obligations such as taxation, accounting, and regulatory compliance.

5. Data sharing & transfers

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data with:

  • Trusted processors (cloud hosting, analytics, communication platforms) under signed Data Processing Agreements.
  • Professional advisors such as accountants, auditors, or legal counsel.
  • Authorities when required to comply with law or to protect rights, safety, or property.

Because Fixare Studio works with clients globally, your information may be transferred across borders. We use industry-standard safeguards like Standard Contractual Clauses and encryption-in-transit/at-rest to protect your data.

6. Retention & security

We keep personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy. Retention periods vary based on project lifecycle, accounting rules, and legal requirements.

Our security program includes least-privilege access, encrypted storage, multi-factor authentication, regular backups, supplier due diligence, and continuous monitoring. No method of transmission is entirely risk-free, but we take all reasonable measures to protect your data.

7. Your rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request corrections, updates, or deletion of your information.
  • Restrict or object to certain processing activities.
  • Receive a portable copy of data you provided.
  • Opt out of marketing communications at any time.

To exercise these rights, email privacy@fixare.studio. We aim to respond within 30 days.

8. Children's information

Our services are intended for business users and are not directed to children under 16. If we discover we have collected personal data from a child without verifiable parental consent, we will delete it promptly.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect operational, legal, or regulatory changes. When we do, we will adjust the "Last updated" date above and, where appropriate, notify you via email or site banner. Your continued use of Fixare Studio services after an update signifies acceptance of the revised terms.

If you have privacy questions, need to file a complaint, or want to request a signed Data Processing Agreement, reach us at:
